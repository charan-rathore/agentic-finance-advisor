# JNJ

## Summary
Johnson & Johnson (JNJ) is a global leader in the [[Healthcare]] sector, encompassing pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and consumer health products. The company is highly regarded for its stability and is often categorized as a [[Defensive Healthcare]] stock, appealing to investors seeking resilient performance during market uncertainty. JNJ's diversified business model and strong cash flows position it as a frequent inclusion in [[Dividend Aristocrats]] portfolios.

## Recent Price Action
As of a recent market update, JNJ traded at **$241.85** with a volume of **12,500,000**.

## News & Sentiment
Johnson & Johnson recently reported strong Q1 earnings, primarily driven by robust pharmaceutical sales and resilience in its MedTech division. Despite these positive results, the company continues to face an overhang from ongoing [[Healthcare Litigation]], particularly related to talc liability, which can temper investor sentiment. Amid broader market uncertainty, JNJ, along with other healthcare giants, is attracting significant interest from defensive investors who value the sector's recession-resistant characteristics and stable earnings potential.

## Key Risks
JNJ faces several material risks that could impact its future performance:
*   Significant [[Healthcare Litigation]] liability, particularly from talc-related lawsuits.
*   Potential impact from [[Patent Cliff Risk]] on key pharmaceutical drugs.
*   Increased [[Biosimilar Competition]] affecting existing drug revenues.
*   Delays in critical FDA approvals for new products or indications.
*   Industry-wide pressure on healthcare pricing from payers and governments.
