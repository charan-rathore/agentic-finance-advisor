# HDFC Index Fund Nifty 50 Plan (Direct Growth)

A plain-vanilla index fund that passively tracks the Nifty 50 Total Return Index.
Index funds are the lowest-cost, most beginner-friendly way to own Indian large-cap
equity.

## How it works

The fund replicates the Nifty 50 by holding all 50 constituents in their index
weights. There is no fund manager picking stocks. Performance closely mirrors
the index minus a small tracking error and the expense ratio.

## Costs (the main reason to choose it)

Direct plan expense ratio is typically 0.20 percent or below. Over 20 years at
12 percent gross return, a 1 percent fee gap compounds to roughly 18 percent of
final wealth lost. Index funds are the most reliable way to capture the entire
historical equity premium.

## Best fit

* First-time equity investors who want one product to start a SIP
* Anyone who has decided not to spend time picking active funds
* Long-term goals (5 plus years), retirement, or generic wealth building

## Limitations

* No alpha by design. The fund cannot outperform the index
* Concentration risk is the same as the index. Top 10 stocks make up about 60 percent
  of the Nifty 50
