# Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund (Direct Growth)

An ELSS fund managed by Mirae Asset Mutual Fund. Together with Axis ELSS and a
handful of others, it is one of the most widely held tax-saver funds in India.

## What makes ELSS the recommended 80C choice for equity-comfortable investors

| Instrument | Lock-in | Risk | Indicative return |
|---|---|---|---|
| ELSS Mutual Fund | 3 years | Equity (high) | 10 to 14 percent historical CAGR |
| PPF | 15 years (partial after 7) | Sovereign | 7.1 percent (Q1 FY26, set by Govt) |
| NSC | 5 years | Sovereign | 7.7 percent |
| Tax-saving FD | 5 years | Bank | 6 to 7 percent |
| ULIP | 5 years | Equity / debt | Varies, charges high |

ELSS has the shortest lock-in among 80C equity products and has historically
delivered the highest long-run return because it is pure equity. PPF is still the
right product for the safety-first sleeve of the 80C bucket.

## Cost

Direct plan expense ratio is typically 0.50 to 0.80 percent. Lower fees on a
long-horizon SIP compound meaningfully over 10 to 20 years.

## When to choose Mirae over Axis

The two funds have different style tilts in different periods. Mirae has, over
its life, leaned toward a slightly higher mid-cap allocation. Axis has historically
been more growth-tilted with quality bias. A reasonable approach is to pick one
ELSS fund and stay with it. Holding two funds in the same 80C bucket adds
complexity without clear diversification benefit.
