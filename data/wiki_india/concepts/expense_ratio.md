# Expense Ratio: The Quiet Number That Decides Your Returns

The expense ratio (ER) is the annual fee a mutual fund charges to manage your
money. It covers fund management, administration, marketing, distributor
commissions (Regular plans only), and transaction costs. It is deducted daily
from the scheme's NAV, not separately from your bank account.

## SEBI cap framework

SEBI caps the maximum permissible expense ratio based on scheme assets under
management. Indicative slabs for equity-oriented schemes:

| Scheme AUM | Max ER, equity |
|---|---|
| First INR 500 crore | 2.25 percent |
| Next INR 250 crore | 2.00 percent |
| Next INR 1,250 crore | 1.75 percent |
| Above INR 50,000 crore | 1.05 percent |

Debt schemes have a tighter cap, typically 25 to 50 basis points lower than
equity. Index funds and ETFs typically charge well below the cap, often 0.10
to 0.40 percent direct plan.

## Real-world ranges (FY 2025-26, indicative)

| Category | Direct plan | Regular plan |
|---|---|---|
| Index fund (Nifty 50) | 0.10 to 0.30 percent | 0.30 to 0.60 percent |
| ETF (Nifty 50) | 0.04 to 0.20 percent | Not applicable, ETFs trade on exchange |
| Active large cap | 0.80 to 1.20 percent | 1.50 to 1.90 percent |
| Active flexi cap | 0.60 to 1.00 percent | 1.30 to 1.80 percent |
| Active small cap | 0.70 to 0.90 percent | 1.60 to 1.85 percent |
| Liquid fund | 0.10 to 0.25 percent | 0.20 to 0.45 percent |

## Why a small ER difference matters

A 1 percent ER gap, compounded over 25 years on an INR 5,000 monthly SIP at
12 percent gross, costs approximately **INR 12 to 14 lakh** of final wealth.
Over a working life, ER is one of the few investment variables you fully
control.

## How to read a factsheet for ER

* Check the **most recent monthly factsheet** on the AMC website
* Look for "Expense Ratio (Direct)" and "Expense Ratio (Regular)"
* Confirm the AMFI scheme code and plan name match what you hold
* Compare against the category median, available on SEBI MF SCORES portal or
  AMFI dashboard

## Beyond ER

ER is not the full cost story. Other costs to watch:

* **Exit load.** A penalty for redeeming within a holding period (typically 1
  percent if redeemed within 365 days for equity)
* **Securities Transaction Tax (STT).** Levied on equity redemptions at 0.001
  percent (very small)
* **Stamp duty.** 0.005 percent on every purchase, including SIP instalments
  (since July 2020)
